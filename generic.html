About Me

Hello there! My name is Tori, I am years old, and I'm based out of Texas! I started cosplaying when I was 16 on and off until 2019. My climb to fame started when I joined the JJBA fandom and began cosplaying Jotaro Kujo from part 3 and 4 and posting videos on TikTok. From there, I grew into a large following where I'm known for my comedic content as well as my makeup skills and my thrifted cosplays. Only as of late 2019 have I started making my cosplays fully handmade. Those cosplays include my Kakyoin, Part 6 Jotaro, and Jolyne, hopefully making more in the future! I also have passions for digital art, fanfiction writing, and voice acting.

This page is for promoting my cosplay as well as providing a way to order prints or art commisions. I have both high-res photo shoots as well as my instagram selfies and edits up for grabs and I am willing to sell any size or style up to an 8 x 10 size.
